Welcome to the Index-ISH. The official ISH Coding Club. Right now, this club is only available to 7th Grade Students. We hope to continute to other grades after this virus :).



Here, you will learn about the basics of coding/programming, and learn to:

  •    • Think differently (different mindset)

  •    • Create AWESOME things with python (e.g a whatsapp messaging robot, and email sender script) , and you will learn to create things
         that help you in your everyday life

  •    • You will learn to be better at everything computer related. And you will get a MUCH better understanding of how a computer works

  •    • You will improve your thinking skills, math skills and more

  •    • You will learn to create BEAUTIFUL applications, that you can share with your friends.

  •    • You will learn to make WEBSITES (not with wix :), with pure HTML, CSS, JS coding) that you can upload and use

  •    • Learn to inspect websites and see how they work

  •    • Much much more things that help you in your life.


All of this you will hopefully be learning at our coding club. Apart from coding, you will have fun messing around with friends, create cool stuff, make really interesting projects.
